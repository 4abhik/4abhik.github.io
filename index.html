My approach

How I work

Every engagement is different, but the underlying approach is consistent: understand the real problem first, then work backwards to the solution.

Diagnose
We start with a structured conversation about your business, your customers, and the gap between where you are and where you need to be. No assumptions, no templates.
Align
I synthesise findings into a clear point of view — what the actual problem is, what levers exist, and what the path forward looks like. We align before we act.
Execute
I work alongside your team in the execution phase — not just advising from the sidelines. Deliverables are concrete, timelines are respected.
Iterate
Markets change, customers surprise you. I stay engaged to review, refine, and course-correct as you learn — because strategy is a living thing.
